# COPADO_ACCOUNT_MIGRATION

## Step-by-Step Instructions to Disconnect an Old Copado Essentials Account from Salesforce and Connect a New Copado Account

### Step 1: Log in to Salesforce
1. Open your preferred web browser.
2. Go to the Salesforce login page.
3. Enter your credentials for the Salesforce account linked to your old Copado Essentials account.

### Step 2: Navigate to Setup
1. Click on the gear icon in the upper right corner of the Salesforce dashboard.
2. Select "Setup" from the dropdown menu.

### Step 3: Disconnect Old Copado Account
1. In the Quick Find box, search for "Installed Packages."
2. Click on "Installed Packages."
3. Find the Copado Essentials package in the list.
4. Click on the package name. 
5. Select "Uninstall" or "Remove". (Please note that this action may affect your current setup. Ensure you have backups if necessary.)
6. Confirm any prompts to complete the uninstallation.

### Step 4: Connect New Copado Account
1. Visit the Copado website.
2. Click on “Sign Up” or “Log In” if you already have an account.
3. Follow the prompts to create a new account or log into your new Copado account.

### Step 5: Install New Copado Essentials Package
1. Once logged in, you will receive a confirmation to install the Copado Essentials package.
2. Click on the provided link to install the package directly into your Salesforce instance.
3. Follow the installation steps as prompted.

### Common Issues
- **Issue: Unable to Uninstall the Old Package**
  - *Solution:* Ensure that all dependencies related to the package are removed first.
- **Issue: New Account Not Linking Properly**
  - *Solution:* Double-check your new Copado account settings and verify the installation of the new package.

## Conclusion
After completing these steps, your Copado Essentials account will be successfully migrated from the old account to the new one! If you encounter issues, consult the Copado support documentation or contact their support team.
